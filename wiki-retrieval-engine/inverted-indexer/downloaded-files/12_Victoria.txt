coincidence with the name of the then reigning queen caused quite a controversy at the time and b a gould editor of the prestigious astronomical journal adopted the alternate name clio now used by 84 klio proposed by the discoverer however w c bond of the harvard college observatory then the highest authority on astronomy in america held that the mythological condition was fulfilled and the name therefore acceptable and his opinion eventually prevailed the path of the main belt asteroid 12 victoria radar and speckle interferometry observations show that the shape of victoria is elongated and
